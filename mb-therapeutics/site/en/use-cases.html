Decision-making Questions

Can we test without modifying existing workflows?
Yes. The pilot is designed to run in parallel with your current flows ("side-by-side") to compare metrics without risk of disruption.
Is the ROI reliable?
Our calculators use your own costs (hourly rates, consumables, time). We only provide the calculation method; you keep control over the assumptions.
What is the average time for a pilot?
Between initial scoping and on-site startup, generally count 8 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity of internal validations.
